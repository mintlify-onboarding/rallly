First, make sure you have access to the [admin page](/administrators/accessing-the-admin-page) of your poll. From here you can access the **Manage** menu.

![](/images/administrators/CleanShot%202023-03-21%20at%2017.45.47.png)

Admin controls

## Changing the title, description or location

To change these details of your poll:

1. 1.  
Click **Manage**

2. 2.  
Select **Edit details** from the dropdown menu

![](/images/administrators/CleanShot%202023-03-21%20at%2017.48.02.png)

1. 3.  
Enter the new details of your poll in to the form and click **Save**.

## Adding or removing dates or times

1. 1.  
Click **Manage**

2. 2.  
Select **Edit options** from the dropdown menu

![](/images/administrators/CleanShot%202023-03-21%20at%2017.51.38.png)

Date or time picker

1. 3.  
Add/remove options using the date/time picker
