To respond to a poll, start by clicking the buttons under the corresponding dates to set your availability.

* Yes (1 click)

* If need be (2 clicks)

* No (3 clicks or leave blank)

![](/images/participants/CleanShot%202023-03-21%20at%2018.32.51.png)

Once you've set your availability click **Continue** to bring up the **New Participant** dialog.

![](/images/participants/CleanShot%202023-03-21%20at%2018.26.40.png)

The New Pariticpant Dialog

Enter your name and your email address in to the form and click **Submit**.
